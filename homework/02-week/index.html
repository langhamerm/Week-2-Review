Chante is stronger and smarter then anyone expects her to be. She can be stubborn as a bull, but only because she is almost always right. Chante has been through more pain than most people can even imagine, but her struggles have made her destructible. Chante is the most loyal friend you'll ever have, and that's why she's loved by those closest to her. Chante is smart, but she doesn't always show it. She knows it gives her advantage when people underestimate her. Chante's only weakness is that she care too much.
